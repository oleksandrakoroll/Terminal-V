About Terminal V

Terminal V is one of the most celebrated and largest techno festivals in Europe, returning to Edinburgh's Royal Highland Centre and Showground on Easter Bank Holiday Weekend 2025. With over 100 world-class acts and six stages, Terminal V offers a supersized experience.
