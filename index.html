About Terminal V

Terminal V is one of the most celebrated and largest techno festivals in Europe, returning to Edinburgh's Royal Highland Centre and Showground on Easter Bank Holiday Weekend 2025. With over 100 world-class acts, six specially designed stages, and 20,000 people per day, Terminal V offers a supersized experience for techno enthusiasts.
